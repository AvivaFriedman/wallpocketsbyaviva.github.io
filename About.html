About Wall Pockets

Once upon a time there was a girl named Sade who had nowhere to put her glasses at night. Her bed was very high up, and she had no nightstand. This problem persisted until her very kind friend Aviva crafted her a Wall Pocket. Suddenly, Sade's evenings were filled with ease rather than frustration. Once their other friends saw Sade's Wall Pocket they asked Aviva if she could make them Wall Pockets too. Each Wall Pocket owner found new and exciting uses for their Pockets, from holding their glasses, to their phones, to their toothbrushes, to their pencils. You too can make your life a little easier by purchasing a Wall Pocket today and finding new and creative places to put them. Visit our Facebook page "Wall Pockets by Aviva" or email WallPocketsbyAviva@gmail.com to order your very own Wall Pocket!

FAQs

Where can I put a Wall Pocket?

Wall Pockets stick to any smooth surface! Put one on your wall, mirror, desk, bedframe, door, etc. Send us a picture of your Wall Pocket so we can feature your unique Pocket placement in our Gallary

Is a Wall Pocket difficult to put up?

No! It super easy! All you do is wipe off the desired surface, peel back the plastic from the back of the Wall Pocket tape, and stick the Wall Pocket to any smooth surface. For best results leave for 24 hours before putting items in your Wall Pocket

How do I buy a Wall Pocket?

Message Wall Pockets by Aviva on Facebook or email WallPocketsbyAviva@gmail.com

Can I design my own Wall Pocket?

Great Question! You have a couple of options for your Wall Pocket design
  • Send us a picture to put on your Wall Pocket
  • Tell us a color or theme you like and we will decorate your Wall Pocket accordingly
